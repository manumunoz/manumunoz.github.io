Social Bee Meet
15 - 16 January, 2026 @ LISER

Welcome

The First Social Behavior and Experimental Economics Meeting (Social Bee Meet) is scheduled for January 15 and 16, 2026, hosted by the Luxembourg Institute of Socio-Economic Research (LISER) in Belval, Luxembourg. This event will bring together researchers using experimental methods to study individual and collective behavior, to present and discuss their latest work. The workshop provides a unique platform for presenters to receive high-quality feedback, fostering a vibrant exchange of ideas among both established and early-career researchers.


The 2026 edition will take place at the Salle de Conference (Jane Jacobs Salle) at the Luxembourg Institute of Socio-Economic Research (LISER). The institute is located in the fascinating town of Belval, where architectural innovation blends with the remnants of a former steel mill, highlighted by its iconic blast furnaces with striking modern structures. This harmonious fusion creates a one-of-a-kind environment that reflects the region's industrial heritage while embracing a vision of contemporary design and creativity. Attendees can look forward to enriching academic discussions in this inspirational setting.
