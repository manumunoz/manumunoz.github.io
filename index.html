I am a behavioral scientist at LISER, and the co-founder and research director of the Social Bee Lab.

I use lab, on-line and field experiments to study how social connections and group identities may lead to inequalities in the access to opportunities. I also evaluate how different policies can help overcome these barriers and promote social and economic mobility. You can see my work (here) and my CV (here).
